Biography

Zhiwei is currently an associate professor at College of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). He got his PhD degree in College of Computer Science, Zhejiang University, China. His co-advisors are Prof. Jiajun Bu and Prof. Wei Dong. He is a former member of EmNets, EAGLE lab (led by Prof. Chun Chen). He received his Bachelor's Degree in School of Electronic and Information Engineering from Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2010. His research interests include wireless and mobile computing, low power and networked systems, edge computing, embedded intelligence, future networks (SDN/NFV), etc.
Our research group can be found here.
